# Privacy Policy – EVIDRA

Last updated: December 2025

EVIDRA is an educational and professional platform intended for medical and pharmacology professionals to share, discuss, and reference scientific evidence.

## Information We Collect
- Account information (such as email or name) provided during sign-in
- User-generated content (posts, comments, evidence references)
- Basic usage analytics to improve app performance

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- No patient-identifiable data
- No medical records
- No biometric or health sensor data
- No financial information

## Use of Information
Collected information is used only to:
- Enable app functionality
- Maintain account security
- Improve user experience

EVIDRA does not provide clinical diagnosis or treatment recommendations.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising purposes.

## Data Security
Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored information.

## Children’s Privacy
EVIDRA is intended for users aged 18 and above and is not directed at children.
